This project is too make a small menu for the nokia 5110 lcd for the arduino.
pin output/input is defined in the source code. this project is open source
so feel free to use it but please give some credit if you do. and definitly 
give credit to those that deserve it.--------------------------------------

requirements* (they can be changed)
3.3 v power for lcd
2 tact switches or other buttons( 1 for movement through menu and 1 for slecting the options)
arduino 
nokia 5110 lcd or 3110
10k potentiometer
wire

*tip its neccesary to connect the 3.3v from the arduino to the lcd but you should use the 5v for 
the switches because it drain power causing the lcd to black out.
